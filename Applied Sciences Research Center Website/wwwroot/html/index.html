Meet Our In-House

Scientists & Expert Council

We work with a lot of exceptional and thoughtful people—like the researchers, professors, and experts on our Zoology Faculty Research Team, who help guide paper development and academic inquiry.

Assistant Professor/Managing Editor
Dr. Najiya al-Arifa, PhD
Assistant Professor / Zoologist

Dr. Najiya al-Arifa is an accomplished academic and researcher, serving as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Zoology at Lahore College for Women University (LCWU), Lahore, Pakistan. With a strong foundation in zoology, Dr. Najiya al-Arifa completed their Ph.D. in Zoology in 2018 from Government College University (GCU), Lahore, after earning their M.Phil. and M.Sc. in the same field. Their research interests lie in immunology, toxicology, and microbiology, focusing on the impact of environmental factors like toxins, allergens, and microbes on the immune system, particularly in relation to asthma and allergies. Dr. Najiya al-Arifa has established an Immunology Research Laboratory at LCWU and has been an influential mentor, guiding numerous students at various academic levels. They have received multiple honors, including the HEC-IRSIP Scholarship, the Fauji Foundation Academic Distinction Award, and various recognitions for academic excellence. Dr. Najiya al-Arifa is also actively involved in departmental administration and social outreach, contributing to initiatives in public health and research.
